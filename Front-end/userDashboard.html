Common Issues

How do I report a damaged bin?

Can I track my complaint?

Yes! You'll receive a complaint ID via SMS/Email that you can use to track the status of your complaint.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I find my collection schedule?
    Go to "Collection Schedule" → Select your Municipal Council and Ward → Click "View Schedule". Your weekly calendar will display all collection days with waste types.
  • What if my waste wasn't collected?
    Submit a "Missed Collection" complaint through the Complaints section. Include your location and scheduled collection day. We'll arrange pickup within 24 hours.
  • Which items can I recycle?
    Clean paper, cardboard, plastic bottles (#1-7), glass containers, and metal cans. Avoid contaminated items, electronics, and hazardous materials in recycling bins.
  • How do I set up collection reminders?
    In your collection schedule, click the bell icon next to any collection day, or use "Enable All Reminders" button to activate notifications for all scheduled pickups.
  • How quickly are complaints resolved?
    High priority issues are resolved within 24 hours, routine matters within 48 hours. You'll receive SMS/email updates throughout the resolution process.
