Common Issues

What if my waste wasn't collected?

Submit a "Missed Collection" complaint with your scheduled collection day and we'll arrange a pickup within 24 hours.

How do I report a damaged bin?

Select "Damaged Bin" category and describe the damage. We'll arrange a replacement within 2-3 business days.

Can I track my complaint?

Yes! You'll receive a complaint ID via SMS/Email that you can use to track the status of your complaint.

Tips for Better Service

Place bins at curb by 6:00 AM on collection day
Separate waste according to collection schedule
Keep bins clean and in good condition
Report issues promptly for faster resolution
